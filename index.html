FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the JB Hi-Fi Corporate Benefits portal?


Simply click the Access JB Hi-Fi Benefits button from Gorilla Dash. This ensures only franchise partners can use the portal.

Can I use JB Hi-Fi gift cards on the portal?

No. Purchases must be completed with a credit or debit card. Gift cards are not accepted through the corporate site.

Why do some products show the normal ticket price?

If an item is already on retail promotion in-store, the corporate portal will match that ticketed price instead of applying an additional discount.

How do I collect an order from a JB Hi-Fi store?

After checkout you’ll receive a Letter of Authority email. Present this at the counter to collect your order. Without it, the store cannot release your items.

Can I get items delivered directly to my store?

Yes. Bulky or larger items may be shipped directly, while standard products are often available for in-store collection. Delivery times are usually 3–7 business days.
